CATERPILLAR C7 DIAGNOSTIC MODEL INTEGRATION NOTES
============================================================

5. Class Labels Mapping for Frontend Display:
   * Section Output Branches:
     0: Healthy (No Leak)
     1: Air Filter to MAF Sensor
     2: MAF Sensor to Turbo Compressor In
     3: Turbo Compressor Out to CAC
     4: CAC to Intake Manifold
     5: Cylinder to Turbine In
     6: Diesel Oxidation Catalyst (DOC)
     7: Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF)
     8: SCR Tracking Zone

   * Severity Output Branches:
     0: No Leak Detected
     1: Low Severity Leak
     2: Moderate Severity Leak
     3: High Severity Leak

   * Go / No-Go Indicator:
     0: NON-GO (IMMEDIATE SHUTDOWN)
     1: GO (System Safe)
